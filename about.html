About Auroflex

Engineering Reliable Expansion Solutions

Auroflex is a specialized manufacturer of high-performance expansion joints and metal bellows designed for industrial piping and ducting systems. Originally established as Rama Engineers in 1998, the company has evolved into a trusted engineering brand delivering reliable solutions for thermal expansion, vibration isolation, pressure balancing, and movement absorption.

Our products are engineered to operate under demanding industrial conditions including high temperatures, pressure variations, corrosive environments, and dynamic mechanical movement. With advanced manufacturing capabilities and strict quality standards, Auroflex serves industries across India and international markets.
