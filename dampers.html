PRODUCT RANGE

Damper Portfolio

Auroflex supplies a complete range of industrial dampers for airflow regulation, duct isolation, and process control across HVAC, power, cement, and heavy industrial duct systems.

01

Manual Dampers

Hand-operated dampers with lever or quadrant control for reliable airflow adjustment in industrial duct lines.

02

Motorised Dampers

Actuator-driven dampers for automated flow control, remote operation, and integration with plant control systems.

03

Butterfly Dampers

Compact rotary-blade dampers suited for large-diameter ducts requiring low-pressure drop and quick shut-off.

04

Guillotine Dampers

Slide-gate dampers designed for positive isolation and tight shut-off in high-temperature flue gas and process ducts.

05

Louver Dampers

Multi-blade louver assemblies for balanced airflow distribution and modulation in air handling and exhaust systems.

06

Diverter Dampers

Three-way and diverter configurations for directing gas flow between alternate duct paths in process and utility systems.
